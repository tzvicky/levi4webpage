About Levi IV

To keep with the tradition of our productive and socially vivid scientific meetings (Levi 2013, Nove Hrady 2015, and Dresden 2016) that were born from Ilpo Vattulainen and Pavel Jungwirth collaboration, we are organizing a new meeting (Levi IV) in Nove Hrady on May 23-26, 2018. This will be kept in the style of a small conference encouraging direct communication in a friendly atmosphere. The topic as before focuses mostly on "Membranes and monolayers and any related process" covering both experimental and computational aspects.

Venue and practical considerations

Venue information

The conference will take place in The Center for Nanobiology and Structural Biology of the Czech Academy of Sciences in Nové Hrady, Czech Republic (map).

Travel information

Nové Hrady and the conference site can be accessed from České Budějovice using a bus. České Budějovice is conveniently accessible from Prague via train.

For those people landing in Viena, a shuttle has been arranged (12 people). Departure from Viena 23rd around 11 am and arrival to Viena 26th around 18 pm.

If you require assistance arriving or leaving the conference venue, please send an email to the organizers (hseara [a] uochb.cas.cz).
